    Install DJI Assistant 2

    Download and run "Consumer Drones Series" installer to bind underlying virtual COM drivers.

  • 2

    Plug in USB-C Cable

    Connect your DJI RC-N1C via the bottom USB-C port to your computer, then turn the controller on.

  • 3

    Auto-Detect & Run

    Close DJI Assistant 2 completely. The bridge script will auto-detect the COM port and map sticks.

  • 4

    Launch Flight Simulator

    Launch Liftoff, DCL, or VelociDrone. Select Xbox Gamepad and calibrate channels!
